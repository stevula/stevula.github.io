Minefield

Directions

Click on a cell to uncover its value.

Safe tiles are marked with numbers. Mines are marked with "X".

The number revealed will tell you how many mines are touching.

If you click on a mine ("X"), you lose.

Uncover all of the safe tiles to win.

Refresh the page to start a new board.
